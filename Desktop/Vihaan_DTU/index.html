Dare to Enter. Pray to Escape. Silence the Shrieks of Madness.

SOSG comprises of:

Immersive VR Experience

Dive into the heart-pounding action with cutting-edge VR technology, allowing players to step into the shoes of a brave operative navigating the terrifying corridors of NovaTech Industries.

Confront Your Fears

Encounter a diverse range of chilling environments and face your deepest fears as you battle against the sinister entity, Shy. From claustrophobic hallways to dark laboratories, every corner holds new horrors to overcome.

Intense Horror Shooting Gameplay

Arm yourself with an arsenal of powerful weaponry and engage in heart-stopping shootouts against hordes of nightmarish creatures spawned by Shy's influence. Precision aiming and quick reflexes are essential to survive the relentless onslaught.

Unravel the Mystery

Delve into the dark secrets hidden within NovaTech Industries as you uncover the twisted experiments of Dr. Elizabeth Nova. Piece together the clues scattered throughout the facility to unveil the truth behind Shy's creation and put an end to its reign of terror.
